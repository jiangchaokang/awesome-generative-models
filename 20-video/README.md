## Research Pattern

```text
Text / image / pose / sketch / camera
        ↓
Condition encoders + video tokenizer / VAE
        ↓
Spatiotemporal generator (DiT / diffusion / flow)
        ↓
Temporal consistency / editing / motion control
        ↓
Video clip / surround-view sequence
```
